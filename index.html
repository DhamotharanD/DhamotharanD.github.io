Hi! I'm Dhamotharan D.

As a Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) enthusiast, I am driven by a fascination with the space where fundamental science meets applied engineering. I aim to build a bridge between these two worlds, grounded in the foundations of chemistry and physics. I'm passionate about exploring how the universe works at the smallest scales and then using that knowledge to build things that have a real-world impact.

At its core, my work is about understanding the "why" behind a material's behavior. I delve into the intricate connections between a material's atomic structure and its resulting electrochemical, electrical, and thermal properties. This focus on structure-property correlations allows me to predict and manipulate the performance of a wide range of materials—from advanced ceramics and semiconductors to novel alloys and polymers. This involves the entire lifecycle of material development, from initial design and synthesis to advanced characterization. Ultimately, my long-term goal is to advance next-generation materials, tackling new challenges to power the upcoming digital era in a sustainable and responsible way.

Research Projects

Rapid Room-Temperature Synthesis of Metal Nitrides

Aug 2025 – Present

Developing a novel synthesis route for phase-pure nitrides using a modified solid-state metathesis reaction, targeting scalable and energy-efficient production.

Guide: Prof. Ravikumar, IIT Madras, India.
This project aims to revolutionize the synthesis of metal nitrides by creating a method that is not only rapid but also operates at room temperature, drastically reducing energy costs. My work involves building the custom reaction setup and demonstrating its viability for producing tunable Zirconium Nitride (ZrN).

Flexible Ceramic-Based Triboelectric Nanogenerators (TENGs)

Jan 2024 – Present

Pioneered research into flexible ceramic TENGs by synthesizing high-entropy oxides and developing a scalable thick-film fabrication methodology.

Guide: Prof. Ravikumar, IIT Madras, India.
I addressed a key challenge in wearable electronics by creating durable, high-performance energy harvesters. I overcame the brittleness of ceramics by synthesizing novel high-entropy oxides and developing a scalable thick-film methodology, a critical step towards commercialization.

Electrocatalytic Green Ammonia Synthesis using LiZnVO4/LiVO3 Nanocomposite

Jun 2024 – Aug 2024

Synthesized novel nanocomposites via a sol-gel route for green ammonia synthesis, achieving 45% Faradaic efficiency and high yield.

Guide: Prof. Sanjay Mathur, University of Cologne, Germany.
During my international research internship, I developed a high-performance electrocatalyst for green ammonia production. This unique biphasic material demonstrated superior catalytic activity, achieving an impressive 45% Faradaic efficiency and a high ammonia yield of 53 µg h-1 mgcat-1.

Electrocatalytic Behaviour of Cu-based Nanowires for Nitrate Reduction

Feb 2023 – Jul 2023

Fabricated and studied various Cu-based nanowires for nitrate sensing, achieving enhanced sensitivity and a low detection limit.

Guide: Prof. Lakshman Neelakantan, IIT Madras, India.
I fabricated various copper-based nanowires within nanoporous templates. Extensive electrochemical testing revealed that the functionalized Cu nanowires exhibited exceptional performance for nitrate sensing, with a high sensitivity of 50 µA/µM and a low detection limit of 10 nM.

Fabrication of Self-Ordered Tunable Nanoporous AAO Templates

Jul 2022 – Dec 2022

Optimized mild and hard anodization processes to create tunable nano-porous AAO templates with improved scalability and reproducibility.

Guide: Prof. Lakshman Neelakantan, IIT Madras, India.
This foundational work was crucial for subsequent nanowire synthesis projects. I refined anodization processes to achieve precise control over pore diameters (20-80 nm) while preventing issues like dielectric breakdown during high-speed growth.

Course Projects

Fabrication and Characterization of Permalloy Thin Films

Jan 2024 – Apr 2024

Deposited and characterized Permalloy thin films, analyzing their structural, morphological, and soft magnetic properties.

This project provided hands-on experience with the complete lifecycle of thin-film materials science, from DC sputtering deposition to comprehensive characterization using XRD, SEM, and VSM to confirm the material's soft magnetic behaviour.

Synthesis and Characterization of InSb Thin Films

Jan 2024 – Apr 2024

Synthesized InSb pellets and deposited thin films, confirming stoichiometry and identifying p-type conductivity due to indium vacancies.

Focusing on a key material for infrared detectors, this project involved the synthesis of InSb pellets and deposition using e-beam evaporation. Electrical characterization revealed the film had p-type conductivity, attributed to unintentional doping caused by indium vacancies created during the process.

Conceptual Design of an Autonomous Fruit-Picking Robot

May 2021 – Jul 2021

Collaborated in a team to develop a 3D CAD model, leading the design of its locomotion system.

As part of the CFI Robotics Club's summer bootcamp, I took the lead on designing the locomotion system for a fruit-picking robot, tackling the critical challenge of navigating rough agricultural terrain with a robust crawler track mechanism.
